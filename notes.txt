Meridian Coffee Collective — Internal Onboarding Notes

Meridian Coffee Collective was founded in 2019 in Hartford by two former
insurance actuaries, Priya Sundaram and Del Okafor. The company runs three
cafes and a small roastery in an old typewriter factory on Capitol Avenue.

Our signature drink is the "Gray Whale" — a double shot of espresso over
oat milk with a pinch of smoked sea salt and maple syrup. It was invented
by accident in 2021 when a barista named Theo grabbed the wrong syrup
bottle during a rush. It now accounts for roughly 30% of all drink sales.

Employee policies: all staff get one free bag of beans per week, chosen
from anything roasted in the previous ten days. Shift swaps must be posted
in the staff app at least 36 hours in advance. The roastery is closed to
non-roasting staff on Tuesdays, when the fire suppression system runs its
weekly test.

The 2026 expansion plan targets two new locations: one in New Haven near
the green, and one inside the Windsor train station. The Windsor location
is contingent on a lease decision expected in September.
